The Right Credentials for Chemical Freight.

Louisiana's petrochemical industry demands more than just a truck. It demands certified drivers, proper placarding, regulatory compliance, and a logistics partner who understands what's at stake with every load.

Beard Logistics entered chemical distribution in 2025 with the full credential stack required to operate in this space — HazMat certification, TSA credentialing, TWIC access, and BlueWave API integration for tanker logistics coordination.
