Our Certifications & Credentials

Every certification Beard Logistics holds was earned intentionally — to open doors, serve more clients, and operate in the most regulated and competitive freight environments in the Gulf South.

Full Credential Portfolio

Certified. Credentialed. Ready.

From diversity certifications to safety and technology credentials — our portfolio spans every dimension of professional logistics operations.

Diversity & Inclusion

DBE / SBE Certified

Disadvantaged Business Enterprise and Small Business Enterprise certified under federal and state programs. Qualifies Beard Logistics for contracts requiring DBE/SBE participation goals.

Why it matters: Government and corporate contracts often require certified diverse suppliers. DBE/SBE opens procurement doors that unlicensed companies can't access.
Diversity & Inclusion

SEDBE Certified

Small Emerging Disadvantaged Business Enterprise certification — Louisiana's program supporting small businesses in public contracting, particularly for transportation and infrastructure projects.

Why it matters: SEDBE certification specifically strengthens eligibility for Louisiana DOTD and local government contracts.
Diversity & Inclusion

Hudson Initiative Certified

Louisiana's Hudson Initiative supports small entrepreneurships owned by Louisiana citizens in state procurement. Certification demonstrates our roots and commitment to Louisiana business.

Why it matters: State agencies and prime contractors use Hudson Initiative certification to meet Louisiana-business participation requirements.
Safety & Security

HazMat Certified

Department of Transportation HazMat certification for the handling, transport, placarding, and documentation of hazardous materials. Required for chemical and petrochemical freight operations.

Why it matters: Without HazMat certification, a logistics company legally cannot handle or arrange transport for regulated hazardous materials. We can.
Safety & Security

TSA Certified

Transportation Security Administration certification for handling security-sensitive cargo and operating within TSA-regulated transportation environments.

Why it matters: TSA certification is required for cargo security programs and many government and defense-related freight operations.
Safety & Security

TWIC Credentialed

Transportation Worker Identification Credential — biometric credential issued by TSA granting access to secure maritime facilities, ports, refineries, and restricted energy infrastructure.

Why it matters: Without TWIC, logistics personnel cannot access port facilities or maritime terminals. Our TWIC credentials enable Gulf Coast and river port operations.
Energy Sector

IADC Member

International Association of Drilling Contractors membership — connecting Beard Logistics to the global drilling and energy industry network for oilfield, offshore, and energy logistics opportunities.

Why it matters: IADC membership signals to energy sector clients that we operate within their professional standards and networks.
Technology & Business Development

Cohart BlueWave API Graduate

Graduate of the Cohart BlueWave API Supplier Development Program — a highly selective program that prepares diverse suppliers for large corporate and government contracting opportunities.

Why it matters: BlueWave API graduation strengthens supplier qualification for major energy and industrial procurement programs.
Business Development

Shell LiveWire Graduate

Graduate of Shell LiveWire — Shell's global entrepreneurship program for energy-sector business leaders. Provides mentorship, resources, and network connections for scaling logistics businesses.

Why it matters: Shell LiveWire graduates are recognized within the energy sector as vetted, growth-stage entrepreneurs — a competitive advantage in industry relationships.

Why This Portfolio Matters

More Credentials. More Doors.

Most independent logistics companies hold one or two certifications. Beard Logistics holds nine — spanning diversity, safety, security, energy sector access, and business development. That breadth isn't accidental.

Founder Henry Beard pursued each credential strategically — to qualify for more contracts, serve more industries, and position Beard Logistics as a serious player in markets that most small logistics companies never reach.

For shippers and prime contractors who need a certified diverse supplier with real operational capability, Beard Logistics is that company.
