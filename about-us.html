About Beard Logistics

Veteran-owned. Louisiana-rooted. Built from the ground up in Baton Rouge since 2012 — and still growing.

Our Mission

Moving Freight. Building Louisiana.

Beard Logistics was founded on a simple idea: logistics should be reliable, responsive, and relationship-driven. As a veteran-owned business, we bring military-grade discipline to every load we manage — precision, accountability, and no excuses.

Our mission is to be the most trusted freight partner in Louisiana by delivering exceptional service every time, creating jobs in our community, and setting the standard for what a veteran-owned logistics company can achieve.

We don't just move freight. We solve problems. When your load is shifted, when your carrier falls through, when you need a warehouse with a forklift at the last minute — that's when you call Beard Logistics.

Our Journey

From Day One to Seven Figures

2012
Beard Logistics Founded

Henry Beard launches Beard Logistics in Baton Rouge, Louisiana — a veteran-owned freight operation built on military discipline and a commitment to getting the job done.

2015
Freight Brokerage License Secured

Beard Logistics becomes a licensed freight broker, expanding our ability to connect Louisiana shippers with a wider carrier network and opening the door to larger accounts.

2022
Cross-Docking & Warehousing Expansion

We expand our warehouse and cross-docking facility near I-10 and I-12 in Baton Rouge — increasing capacity and positioning us at the center of Gulf South freight movement.

2025
Chemical Distribution Launched

Beard Logistics launches compliant chemical distribution services, serving Louisiana's petrochemical corridor with certified drivers and full DOT regulatory compliance.

Today
Seven-Figure Operation & Growing

Beard Logistics now operates as a seven-figure freight and logistics company, serving regional businesses, national retailers, and government agencies across the Gulf South and beyond.

By the Numbers

A Track Record That Speaks

Over more than a decade, Beard Logistics has built a reputation as Louisiana's go-to logistics partner — especially when the situation is urgent and the stakes are high.

We've handled Walmart load restacks, emergency freight reroutes, chemical hauls, and cross-docking operations for some of the Gulf South's biggest shippers. Every job, big or small, gets our full effort.
