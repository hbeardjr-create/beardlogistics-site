Our Journey

Built from one truck into a multi-division logistics company

Henry Beard built Beard Logistics through hands-on service, disciplined execution and a willingness to solve the difficult jobs others avoid.

The company now supports transportation, ACME terminal operations, freight brokerage, warehousing, cross-docking, freight recovery and chemical distribution.

Company timeline

2012

Ready Set Tow begins

Henry Beard purchased his first truck and trailer and began building the operating experience that would become the foundation of Beard Logistics.

2016

Beard Logistics launches

The company launched as a Landstar agency, expanding access to nationwide capacity and broader transportation solutions.

2018

ACME Baton Rouge terminal acquired

The acquisition strengthened oil, gas, petrochemical, pipeline and specialized transportation capabilities across the Gulf Coast.

2019

Independent freight broker authority

Beard Logistics gained greater flexibility to source capacity and design transportation solutions across multiple networks.

2022

Warehouse and cross-dock expansion

The Baton Rouge facility added cross-docking, transloading, storage, rework and freight recovery support.

2025

Chemical distribution division

The company expanded into chemical distribution to support industrial and commercial customers across Louisiana and the Gulf South.

Today

Still growing

Beard Logistics serves industrial companies, national corporations and government customers with one standard: dependable execution and accountable service.
