Industries We Serve

From big-box retail to offshore energy, Beard Logistics provides credentialed, reliable freight and logistics solutions for Louisiana's most important industries.

Who We Work With

Built for the Gulf South's Biggest Sectors

Our combination of freight brokerage, warehousing, chemical logistics, and a deep credential portfolio makes us a versatile partner across multiple industries.

Retail & Big-Box

We handle freight for major retailers including Walmart — from inbound shipments to cross-dock transfers and load restacks. When a load gets rejected, we fix it and get it back on schedule.
