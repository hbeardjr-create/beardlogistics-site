Freight Recovery & Rework — Fast.

When freight goes wrong — shifted pallets, rejected loads, missed pickups — Beard Logistics responds fast and fixes it. Baton Rouge's trusted freight recovery team.

What We Do

We Don't Make Excuses. We Fix It.

Freight problems happen. Pallets shift in transit. Loads get rejected at the receiving dock. Carriers fall through. Whatever the situation, Beard Logistics is the team you call when you need the problem solved — not discussed.

We've handled everything from Walmart load restacks to emergency reroutes on time-sensitive chemical shipments. Our team responds fast, communicates clearly, and gets your freight moving again.

Same-day response is standard. Documentation and photos provided on every recovery job.

Common Situations We Handle

Freight Recovery Use Cases

If your freight is stuck, rejected, shifted, or stranded — there's a good chance we've handled it before.

Walmart & Big-Box Load Restacks

Non-compliant stacking rejected at the Walmart DC or other major retailer? We restack, rewrap, and get the load back on schedule — fast.

Shifted Pallets in Transit

Freight that shifted during transport needs to be assessed, stabilized, and restacked before it can continue. We handle the full rework on-site or at our Baton Rouge facility.

Dock Rejections

Load rejected at the receiving dock for packaging, labeling, or stacking issues? We bring the load back to our facility, fix what's needed, and redispatch.

Emergency Carrier Failure

Your carrier didn't show. The load is sitting and the clock is ticking. We find a replacement and get the freight moving — same day when possible.

Damaged Freight Rework

Transit damage that can be salvaged — broken packaging, loose product, moisture damage — we assess the load and rework what can be saved.

Emergency Rerouting

Load needs to go to a different destination than originally planned? We coordinate the reroute, find capacity, and keep you updated every step of the way.
